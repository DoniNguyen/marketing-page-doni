The Beginning

The team behind DIY Tracker are all students currently attending Lambda School. DIY Tracker was originally a project assigned by the school only for a grade but quickly became the teams own passion project and working on this project brought them together with their passion for creating.

Our Ultimate Goal

If there are two traits that allowed humanity to thrive, it's curiousity and the ability to create things. Here at DIY Tracker, our ultimate goal is to bring people together by giving everyone the opportunity to create things, and share that process with others so that others may recreate said thing or put their own creative spin on it.
