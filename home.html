What is DIY Tracker?

DIY Tracker is an arts and crafts tracking website that also allows its users to share their project with others.

How does it work?

Users can see your project in progress as you continue to update it on a step by step basis.

Why should i use it?

DIY Tracker helps users stay organized with their projects and can help users stay motivated by allowing them to see their own progress.
